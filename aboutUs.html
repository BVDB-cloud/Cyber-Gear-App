About Us

At Cyber Gear, our products and culture are expertly crafted to ensure an exceptional experience, fostering creativity, collaboration, and growth.

Our Mission: Empowering Organizations with Cutting-Edge Technology

At Cyber Gear, we believe in not just advancing technology, but advancing together. Our mission is to empower organizations to reach new heights by aligning their success with the success of their clients. With our innovative solutions, we create a future where everyone wins.

Our Story

In 2020, innovative engineers Mike Weng, David Lee, and Ben Van Den Bossche identified a transformative shift in how businesses leveraged technology. Organizations required not just software, but intelligent, seamless solutions that could propel them forward. In 2022, they founded Cyber Gear to help companies embrace this change with state-of-the-art, intuitive technology. Over the years, Cyber Gear has evolved beyond its initial offerings, developing a meticulously engineered suite of products designed to boost operational efficiency and foster innovation. Under the expert guidance of CEO Alex Thompson, Cyber Gear employs its advanced AI-powered solutions to deliver a cohesive, frictionless experience, empowering organizations to achieve their highest potential. Today, Cyber Gear proudly supports thousands of enterprises in their journey to grow smarter and more effectively.
